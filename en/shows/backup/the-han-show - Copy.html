The Han Show

“An innovative and thrilling celebration of the traditional Han culture”

The Han Show is a celebration of the essence of the Han culture in Wuhan, China. The show features international artists that will awe with dramatic and thrilling performances unlike anything ever staged.

The Han Show Theater has forever changed the cityscape of Wuhan. Designed by world-renowned architect Mark Fisher and his team at Stufish, the company he founded, the theatre’s architecture is inspired by the “Red Lantern”. The magnificent building is 60 meters high, and 100 meters in diameter and showcases an exterior spectacle on its shell as it dances with light every evening.

The Han Show is Dragone’s most ambitious entertainment endeavour and promises to delight patrons with unprecedented technical innovations and artistic feats. Giant screens mounted and manoeuvred by the largest robot arms ever made will work in tandem with the many surprises that await the 2000 spectators on opening night.
